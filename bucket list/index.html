Forbidden City in Beijing China

Even though it says Forbidden, I'd love to be able to travel to China and see this ancient place!

See Machu Picchu

This wonder has always been a huge interest to me since I was small. I would love to go see this place in person. I wouldn't know how to act.

See The Coliseum in Rome

Being able to see one of the most ancient places in the world would amaze me. Su're it's run down a bit but it's still there after all these years! "Are you not entertained?!"

See The Northern Lights

I would love to be able to travel up north enough to witness the lights. I think this may be without a doubt the coolest thing that I can imagine. To be able to see them in person - I would probably pass out.

Stand in the Sistine Chapel

I can't wait until I can walk inside here and look around. I know pictures are not really allowed, but I would love to be able to snap a few shots of this masterpiece.

Visit Ireland

This is one place on Earth that I will have to visit, no questions asked - some of my roots are from here, so it would only make sense to go there!

Visit Star Wars at Disneyland

This isn't one of the Wonders of the World, and even though it's brand new, it definitely has my attention. To be able to tour the inside of the Millenium Falcon and to make my own lightsaber? That would be so great!

Visit Stonehenge

Being able to go see this monument in person would be without a doubt amazing.

Visit Dracula's Castle in Romania

Okay, admit it - this would be one of the coolest places to go see. Albeit it's probably gloomy as heck on some days, I'd still love to tour this crazy place.

Tour The Vampire Diaries sets in Georgia

Guilty pleasure? maybe. This place wouldn't be nearly as cool as most of my list to many people, sure - but I would be beside myself to be able to go see these places.
